The Reality

Children are suffering in silence.

In Dowa, Lilongwe, and across Malawi, children face early marriage, gender-based violence, harassment, and abuse. Many don't know where to speak. Many don't know who to trust.

42%

of girls in Malawi are married before age 18 — one of the highest rates in the world.

1 in 5

girls experience sexual violence before age 18. Most never report it.

Our Approach

Not just awareness. Real protection.

We don't just talk about gender equality. We build systems that protect children and empower communities to lead.

Gender-Responsive Pedagogy

We train teachers to identify and challenge their own gender biases. How to call on girls equally. How to avoid stereotyping subjects.

Child Complaint Boxes

A simple locked box. A trained response team. A promise: every report is handled seriously, confidentially, and immediately.

Community Ownership

We engage chiefs, PTAs, parents, and religious leaders. Protecting children is everyone's responsibility.
