Pillar 01

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene

We construct solar-powered boreholes, establish Water Point Committees, and deliver hygiene education to eliminate waterborne disease and restore dignity — especially for women and girls who previously walked hours for contaminated water.

Gender Equality & Inclusion

Funded by UNESCO, our gender program creates safe learning environments, establishes gender clubs, and trains teachers and community leaders to tackle the root causes of dropout and inequality in Malawi's central region.
