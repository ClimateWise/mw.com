About Us

Our main goal is to promote sustainable development for a resilient future.

ClimateWise Organization is a non-profit dedicated to fostering environmental sustainability and registered in Malawi. Founded in 2020, we work across sectors such as sustainable agriculture, renewable energy, biodiversity conservation, quality education, gender equality, climate resilience and Water, sanitation and hygiene. Our initiatives focus on empowering communities, innovations, promoting gender equality, and driving socio-economic growth.

Our Reach

Working Together for a Sustainable Future

We operate in nothern, central and southern regions across Malawi, addressing critical challenges and creating sustainable solutions for communities in need.

Lilongwe

Urban reforestation initiatives are restoring green spaces and reducing carbon footprints in Malawi's bustling capital.

Learn More

Our reforestation program in Lilongwe focuses on planting 10,000 trees across the city. The initiative reduces urban heat, absorbs CO2, and enhances biodiversity while creating job opportunities.

View Annual Report Download Annual Report

Ntcheu (Southern)

Providing clean water access to over 20,000 people through borehole construction and improved sanitation systems.

Learn More

Our WASH (Water, Sanitation and Hygiene) program in Ntcheu has transformed lives through:

  • Construction of 15 new boreholes
  • Community hygiene education programs
  • Improved sanitation facilities for schools
