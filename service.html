Integrated Solutions for Sustainable Development

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene (WASH)

We implement comprehensive WASH programs ensuring sustainable access to clean water, improved sanitation facilities, and hygiene education. Our approach reduces waterborne diseases by 60% and enhances community health resilience.

Key initiatives:

  • Borehole drilling and water point rehabilitation
  • Community-led total sanitation (CLTS) programs
  • School WASH facilities with menstrual hygiene management
  • Hygiene behavior change communication

Food Security & Sustainable Livelihoods

We build resilient agricultural systems through climate-smart techniques that increase yields by 40% while conserving natural resources. Our integrated approach connects farmers to markets and diversifies income sources.

Core components:

  • Climate-resilient crop varieties
  • Conservation agriculture training
  • Solar-powered irrigation systems
  • Farmer cooperatives and market linkages
  • Nutrition-sensitive agriculture

"ClimateWise helped us triple our soybean yields using simple water harvesting techniques. We now earn enough to send all children to school."

- Grace Mwale, Farmer Group Leader

Climate Resilience & Ecosystem Restoration

We support communities to thrive despite climate shocks. Our approach blends science with tradition—using trees, water, and local knowledge to protect people and land from climate-related disasters.

From forests to farms, we work together to restore balance between people and nature—so future generations inherit more, not less.

We’ve restored over 1,200 hectares through tree planting with local communities. Our agroforestry systems combine native trees with crops, boosting soil health and family income.

Impact: 60% less soil erosion, 30% more water held in the land.

We support early warning systems, flood-safe farming zones, and train local emergency teams to prepare for floods and droughts before they happen.

Education, Youth & Women Empowerment

We believe strong communities grow from smart ideas and fair chances. That’s why we support young people and women with knowledge, confidence, and the tools they need to create change where they live.

From school clubs to business training, our programs open doors—not just for today, but for a better tomorrow. We don’t just teach—we walk beside them as they plant, lead, and grow.

Schools Program

Hands-on activities and environmental lessons in over 45 schools across Malawi.

Youth Leadership

We train young people in eco-friendly businesses like tree nurseries, clean energy, and waste recycling.

Teacher Training

Over 350 teachers trained to bring climate knowledge into the classroom in creative ways.

Women’s Empowerment

We help women start small green businesses, access savings groups, and gain financial skills with dignity.
